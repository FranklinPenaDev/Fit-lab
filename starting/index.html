About our group

It all started back in 2024 when Joe posted a bulletin looking for a partner to workout with.

The rest is history! We keep growing by the day helping others find accountability partners for your next workout.

Event #1

Dou Workout

Pair up with someone to workout with.

Be sure to track your workouts!

Event #2

Team Weighting Competition

Grab your team of 4 and compete to see which team can lift themselves to 1st place.

Be sure to grab your weightlifting shoes, belt and other accessories!

Event #3

Group HIIT class

In this HIIT class you will have your heart racing and sweat dripping.

Don't forget your towels!
